$ initial client report

Target Users: The main focus of the website will be helping students plan their meals accordingly. The target users are mostly students who maybe live in dorms and/or student housing and struggling with their meal preps. Since most students own laptops and are comfortable with using it they are more likely access our web page through desktop.

Static Information: Nutrition tips and how to have a balanced meal will be added as static information. Most of the time people are not informed about nutrition facts which causes them to get more fat/protein than they might need.

Design Elements: Our client wants to have big pictures, pale tones, and minimalistic feel overall. She does not want the website to be harsh on the eyes.

Data: Generating most popular receipes based on user’s ratings. Collecting people’s preferences for nutritional facts and allergies.

Simple Interactive Elements: Our client wants to match people based on their food preferences and favourite foods. This system will be almost like having a penpal who you can talk to about food and your nutrition goals.
